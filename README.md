# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition trends using Power BI. The objective of the analysis is to identify the major factors affecting employee turnover and provide actionable business insights to improve workforce retention.

The dashboard helps HR leadership understand attrition patterns across departments, job roles, income groups, overtime categories, and work-life balance levels.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- Excel / CSV Dataset

---

## Key Business Insights
- Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition rates.
- Sales and Research & Development departments experience higher employee turnover.
- Lower monthly income employees are more likely to leave the organization.
- Employees with 0–5 years of experience show the highest attrition trends.
- Poor work-life balance contributes to increased employee exits.
